# Day 1 Notes

## Introduction to Salesforce

Today, I explored the basics of Salesforce and understood why it is one of the most widely used CRM platforms in the world.

Salesforce is a cloud-based platform that helps businesses manage customer relationships, sales processes, support services, marketing activities, and overall business operations in a centralized system.

---

## What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

A CRM system helps companies organize customer information, track interactions, manage communication, and improve customer satisfaction. Instead of storing data in multiple places, CRM keeps everything connected in one platform.

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

Businesses use Salesforce because it helps them:

- Store and manage customer data efficiently
- Track leads, contacts, and sales opportunities
- Improve communication with customers
- Automate repetitive business tasks
- Generate reports and dashboards for analysis
- Enhance customer support and service management

Salesforce makes business processes more organized and data-driven.

---

## Understanding Objects in Salesforce

An object in Salesforce is similar to a table in a database.

Objects are used to store specific types of information.

For example:
- A Student object stores student-related data
- A Course object stores course information

Objects contain records and fields.

---

## Understanding Records

A record represents a single entry inside an object.

For example:
- One student's complete information inside the Student object is considered one record.

Each record stores related data together.

---

## Understanding Fields

Fields are similar to columns in a database table.

They define the type of information stored inside a record.

Examples of fields:
- Student Name
- Roll Number
- Email
- Department
- Phone Number

Fields help organize and structure data properly.

---

## Role of a Salesforce Administrator

A Salesforce Administrator is responsible for managing and configuring the Salesforce environment.

Main responsibilities include:
- Managing users and permissions
- Creating objects and fields
- Configuring reports and dashboards
- Automating workflows
- Maintaining data security and system setup

Admins mainly work with configuration rather than coding.

---

## Role of a Salesforce Developer

A Salesforce Developer builds custom functionalities and business solutions on the Salesforce platform.

Developers use technologies such as:
- Apex
- SOQL
- Lightning Web Components (LWC)
- APIs
- Visualforce

Developers help extend Salesforce capabilities based on business requirements.

---

## Overall Understanding

Today’s learning helped me understand the basic structure of Salesforce, including how data is stored using objects, records, and fields.

I also learned the difference between the responsibilities of a Salesforce Admin and a Salesforce Developer, along with the importance of CRM systems in real-world businesses.
